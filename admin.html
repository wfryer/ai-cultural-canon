AI Cultural Canon – Admin

Secure admin panel to manage categories and people in the canon.

Sign In

Use the Firebase Email + Password account configured in your Firebase Authentication settings.

For security, only authenticated users can write to Firestore. Public visitors can read categories and people, but cannot modify them.

Manage Canon Data

After signing in, use the panels below to manage categories and people records. Changes appear immediately in the public explorer.

Categories

High-level groupings such as "Foundational Figures" or "AI Ethics & Governance".

People

Each person belongs to one category and includes an optional image, YouTube link, and Markdown bio.
